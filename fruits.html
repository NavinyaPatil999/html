Fresh Fruits from Around Shirpur!

Mangoes (आम)

Mangoes are a beloved fruit, especially during the summer months in Maharashtra. Known for their sweet taste and vibrant color, they come in many varieties.

Image of a common mango variety.

Bananas (केळी)

Bananas are a widely cultivated and consumed fruit. They are a great source of energy and nutrients.

Image of bananas.

Guava (पेरू)

Guavas are sweet and slightly tart fruits that are rich in Vitamin C. They can be eaten raw or used in making jams and jellies.

Image of guavas.

Pomegranates (डाळिंब)

Pomegranates are known for their juicy, ruby-red seeds that are both delicious and packed with antioxidants.

Image of a pomegranate.

Watermelon (टरबूज)

Watermelons are refreshing and hydrating fruits, perfect for the hot climate. They are large and have a sweet, juicy flesh.

Image of a watermelon slice.
