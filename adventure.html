Awesome Animal Facts!

Did You Know...?

Get ready for some amazing tidbits from the animal kingdom!

Sharks:
Sharks don't have bones! Their skeletons are made of cartilage, the same flexible tissue found in your nose and ears. This helps them move incredibly fast through the water.
Octopuses:
An octopus has three hearts! Two pump blood through its gills, and the third circulates blood to the rest of its body. They also have blue blood because it contains copper.
Snails:
A snail can sleep for three years! They do this when the weather is too hot or dry to survive outside, retreating into their shells for a long nap.
Penguins:
Penguins can drink saltwater! They have a special gland above their eyes that filters out the salt from the water they ingest, allowing them to excrete it through their beaks.
Butterflies:
Butterflies taste with their feet! Their tiny sensors allow them to "taste" whether a leaf is a good place to lay their eggs.
Elephants:
Elephants are the only animals that can't jump. While they're incredibly strong, their leg muscles and ankle structures aren't designed for jumping.

More Animal Wonders!

  • A group of owls is called a **parliament**.
  • Giraffes have the same number of neck vertebrae as humans: **seven**! But each one is much, much longer.
  • Honeybees can fly about **20 miles per hour**.
  • The **chameleon's tongue** is twice as long as its body.
  • The **longest recorded flight** of a chicken is 13 seconds.

Facts compiled for your enjoyment. The animal kingdom is truly fascinating!
